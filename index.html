How is the game?

Everyday a new question will be asked, for example: "What is the name of the band?"

Try to guess the answer! during the day, we will give tips to help you out. But watchout! after a tip goes out, the guess's price doubles, so trying to guess before tips is more cheaper.

How much will i pay?

The guess price varies during the day:
Before any tip: 0.05 ETH;
After first tip: 0.1 ETH;
After second tip: 0.2 ETH;
After third tip: 0.4 ETH.

And, of course, you will pay some gas to send your guess, which go to the miners, so they can process the transaction in the blockchain.

How can i win?

In the end of countdown, the game will be paused and the right answer will be revealed. Everyone who guessed the right answer will receive part of the total amount of this game. For example: If 10 people hit the answer, the total amount (minus 10%) will be divided by these 10 people.

I won! How can i get my prize?

Every winner will receive their part in their wallet (the same used to guess).

Blockchain Security and transparency!

All transactions and calculations are made automatically in the blockchain providing security and transparency.

How to play?

We recommend you installing Metamask. Metamask is a secure digital wallet directly in your browser. Or you can use any Ethereum digital wallet you may prefer

Install Metamask

After that you will need to get some ethers(ETH) in your digital wallet. Ether is the cryptocurrency that powers the Ethereum blockchain.

With Metamask:

All you need to do is input your guess in home page and click Guess! button. Metamask will ask you to confirm the transaction. Click ok.

Without Metamask:

If you are running a Ethereum node like Geth, it means you have a node running on your computer. You can use the web application as well.

Rules:
